Photo Feature — Consent Required

Campistry Link uses facial recognition to automatically find and deliver photos of your child from camp. Before we can enable this feature, we need your consent.

What we do:
• You upload a reference photo of your child
• Our AI scans daily camp photos to find matching faces
• Only photos featuring your child are shown to you
• Your child's facial data is never shared or sold
• You can opt out at any time from Settings
This feature involves processing biometric data of a minor. Your explicit consent is required by law in several US states.
